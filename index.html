About Us

Transforming Education, Empowering Futures: Discover Our Vision for Excellence

Confederation of Education Excellence (CEE) is a newly launched forum aimed at enhancing the quality of education in India. Through initiatives focused on quality education, global outreach, societal commitment, and competitive training, CEE seeks to bridge gaps in the education system and prepare students for success in an increasingly competitive and interconnected world. By collaborating with educational institutions, government bodies, and other stakeholders, CEE aims to elevate the standard of education and facilitate global networking opportunities for institutes in India.CEE is driven by the vision of providing holistic integration of the education sector with the evolving needs of society. With a multi-pronged strategy for global networking and a commitment to fostering social welfare, CEE endeavors to empower students and educational institutions to thrive in a rapidly changing educational landscape.


The common factor in all its activities is partnership across a cross-section of organizations, including government, non-governmental organizations and international bodies. Working with and learning from diverse institutions, CEE has an aim to strengthen the services it provides to its members as well as society at large. CEE aims to works towards providing our membership clearer based on deeper insights and analytical perceptions of emerging realities from all corners of education. At the forefront in helping Indian Institutions to grow with the quality education and interact with global Institutions, CEE has activated a multi-pronged strategy for global networking. Meeting heads, decision makers of academic institutions, networking with counterpart organizations, multilateral and other policy making bodies, CEE’s alternate diplomacy ensures continued access to emerging opportunities for its membership. Through its initiatives, CEE aims to catalyze positive transformations in the Indian education system and contribute to the nation's progress and development.
