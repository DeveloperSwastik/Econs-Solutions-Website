Activities

Activity of The Year 2019-2020

In February 2019, CEE marked the achievement of the year by introducing Robotics based teaching of STEM education at municipal schools in Delhi as the pilot project. Around 4,000 students of MCD schools were trained through a module of STEM education, the outcome of the project was acknowledged and appreciated by the principals of the schools and the Deputy commissioner NDMC, Ms. Vedika Reddy during her personal visit to the school.

On 6th March 2019, Two Day Cancer and preventive health Awareness program was organised by CEE for 200+ teachers and principals of North MCD Schools. The talk was delivered by the experts from Rajiv Gandhi Cancer Hospital and All India Institute of Ayurveda, Ministry of Ayush. The event was graced by the presence of deputy commissioner of North MCD.

In July 2019, To get the citizens of India value their citizenship and have the pride of being, the part of the nation a, campaign was launched by CEE by the name nation pride "Main Hoon Bharat", The campaign aim is to reach 1 lakh oath takers in a year's span.

On 30th November 2019, 600 MCD School Students appear for super 50 Competition organised by Shiv Nadar Foundation, supported by CEE 30th November 2019 in Delhi.

On 12th December 2019, Ganrajya Nirmaan, Phase 2 Campaign of Main Hoon Bharat was launched on 12th Dec 2019 by Sh. Avinash Rai Khanna, BJP Vice President at CCRT, Ministry of Culture in the presence of 500 students and teachers.

In May 2020, Main Hoon Bharat Campaign Successfully Completed One Year With More Than 2 LAKH Oath Takers From 14 Different States Of Bharat.
