International Market

Dewan Tyres was launched in 1972 with its flagship brand of USHA TYRES in the autospace.
We at Vidya Sagar Dewan Tyres believe in delivering our core values : Safety, Comfort, Quality and Durability to all our stakeholders and this is evident from our established export network to various countries such as Nigeria, Egypt, Bangladesh, Srilanka, Bahrain, Holland under the flagship of Usha Tyres, the legacy of which is now carried forward.
The company works according to the international norms and also focuses on working towards a more cleaner and safer environment through its manufacturing process which includes Water scrubber boiler and dust collector plants installed at the production units.
Vidya Sagar Dewan Tyres continue to strive and expand its footprints in the overseas market, motivated by the positive feedback from its existing buyer network across the globe
We take the privilege to appraise that our products are now only being internationally marketed by M/s NANDIKA EXPORTS, 62 Shivaji Road, Meerut 250001, which is our sister concern.
