Activities

Activity of The Year 2018-2019

In April 2018, CEE had taken transformation project and adopted 4 MCD Primary Schools in Delhi for the transformation and Development. The aim to adopt the schools was to equip the infrastructure at par with model school and ensured that the students get all the benefits to become a good citizen in future.

1st June-31st July 2018, Vedic Education Program was conducted at many different schools and Institutions throughout the month. Almost 4000 students and teachers were trained to learn the Sutra of Maa Saraswati.

14th August 2018 For the first time a grand Celebration of Independence Day was carried out in the august presence of the Member of Parliament Dr Udit Raj at MCD School, Rohini, sector 3, adopted by CEE. The school was completely transformed by renovating the infrastructure. Students were encouraged to participate and perform in front of the Ministers and Parents which upscaled their confidence at the highest level.

In October 2018, To felicitate the excellent work done by the selective educationists across the country, CEE organised an education conclave supported by Econs at USI of India Delhi. Around 200 educationists were acknowledged by the BJP Vice President and Chief Guest of the event Sh. Shyam Jaju. CEE along with All India Institute of Ayurveda, Ministry of Ayush co-organised the marathon "Run for Ayurveda" at Jawahar Lal Nehru Stadium. Around 5000 people participated in the marathon and winners were acknowledged and rewarded by by the minister of ayush Sh. Yashopati Naik, BJP vice president Sh. Dushyant Gautam and president CEE Ms. Shruti Arora National CEE along with AIIA, Ministry of Ayush supported the conference of "Invest in Ayurveda and Entrepreneurship at Ambedkar Bhawan Delhi. The event was witnessed by the august presence of the Minister of Culture Sh. Mahesh Sharma, Minister of Ayush Sh. Yashopati Naik and other international guests. The conference was focussed to invite investment opportunities in Ayurveda.
