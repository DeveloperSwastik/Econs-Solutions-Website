Vijaibhav

Vijaibhav: A Portal for Educationists

CEE has always been proactive and concerned for all the matters, issues & difficulties faced by all the concerns ie teachers, students, institutes. In order to conquer this difficulty we felt the necessity and need to overcome it, by finding a solution through a single window basis by launching an online portal VIJAIBHAV. It is presumed that this would fill the existing vacuum of the education field for all concerns.
