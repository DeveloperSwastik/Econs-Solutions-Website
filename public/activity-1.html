Activity of The Year 2014-2015

Since CEE has been launched it is climbing the ladder of success steadily which can be rated by the positive and overwhelming response it has received from various emphasises and government departments apart from different reputed educational institutions at global level.

The first education summit was organized on 26th April 2014, in Delhi, which was focussed on quality in education. The summit was graced by the august presence of host dignitaries from the field of education as vice chancellors from government technical universities and famous private universities.

On 6th September 2014, Teachers Excellence Awards was organized with an aim of rewarding and appreciating the excellent work done by the teachers from school to university at Pan India level. The educational fraternity who attended the function got the chance to witness the presence of our chief guest "Professor YASH PAL â€“ Padma Vibhushan" who is known for his outstanding contribution to science and space technology. His Excellency Mr. MILOSLAY STASEK -the Ambassador of the Czech republic of india and Mr. Gustavo Makanapy cordoba counsellor for Economics and co-operation from embassy of Colombia of India also shared the dias along with vice-chancellors of government and private universities.

In September 2014 workshop for the teachers of government school was conducted on communication skill, confidence building, positive attitude, team building etc. A career counselling session for class 12th students from government schools was organized by CEE, where different options were discussed In detail, keeping in mind the various opportunities for their future employability. The main focus was on 'Girl Child'.

From October 24th to 4th November 2014 CEE in association with Embassy of Czech Republic co-hosted a series of events in major cities of India, where best universities of Czech and Europe Sector's represented their university. As a part of CSR initiative of Intel Corporation, CEE organized Intel Aptitude proficiency test at YWCA, Delhi in October 2014. The institutions at Pan India Level participated where students from 12-18 years were judged on the IT skills by on line and off line test at state and National level. The top winners were felicitated by Padma Bhushan Smt DEVAKI JAIN, a well know Economist.

In April 2015, Education Excellence Summit and Awards function was hosted where the emphasis was on 'Quality in Education and Women Entrepreneurship'. It was Co- sponsored by National skill Development Corporation. (N.S.D.C) which gave a clear view on upcoming strategies of government on skilled Education and Employment. The eminent speakers from the field of education gave their views on Major reforms in Education system 'and 'Role of School and Institute in character Building of the future citizen'. Mr. PRAVEEN DESHMUKH, Parliament Secretary, Delhi government and Mr. RAMESH KAPOOR, Chairman ( steel Bird Group) graced the occasion and appreciated for outstanding performances of individuals and institutions in academic field.
