Activities

Activity of The Year 2017-2018

On 22nd April 2017, A national education conference was held on " Education 2025: Students first", the conference was chaired by the central ministers and educationists across nations.

On June - July 2017, A pilot project for emphasizing practice of meditation at institutional level was organized in various institutions in Delhi and nearby places. The 3 days capsule sessions was delivered by International trainers which was widely appreciated by institutions- Mann Public School, Technia Group of Institution, Dewan Group of Institution, Modern Public School. Almost 3000 students and teachers were trained during this program.

On 2nd September 2017, The Conclave was hosted by Confederation of Education Excellence. In the conclave Honorable Shri Kaptan Singh Solanki, Governor of Haryana graced the occasion as the Chief Guest of the event, this conclave was also witnessed by the Special Guest of honour Shri Manish Sisodia, Deputy Chief Minister of Delhi.

On 28th November -Yamuna Championship Trophy, CEE was associated with Delhi unit of BJP to make the youth outshine and performs in the Yamuna challenge trophy competition organised by the sports' Department of central government. The aim of conducting the event was to emphasis more on practicing physical activities and also giving a platform to the talented youth and young players to get acknowledgement by the department. The event lasted for a week where many young players from Delhi and NCR participated with full enthusiasm. All MPS' from Delhi Including Delhi BJP Chief Manoj Tiwari were present during the final tournament to encourage the teams and presented the cash prize to the winning team.

One Nation One Election -20th and 21th January 2018, One Nation One Election seminar was conducted in Mumbai where CEE supported it as an academic partner. This seminar witnessed the presence of august gathering and eminent personalities from Research Council for political leaders. The seminar was focussed on research paper presentation where researchers presented their views in favour of One Nation, One Election.

In March 2018, Emphasising move to have peace and harmony in the country, CEE in association with Shree Yogini Peetham organised Rashtra Raksha Mahayagya.
