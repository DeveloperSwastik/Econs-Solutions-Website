Activities

Activity of The Year 2016-2017

On 23rd April 2016, Hosted Education summit on Quality in Education and women entrepreneurship, along with NSDC (Government of India Initiative) Dr. Udit Raj, Member of Parliament Lok Sabha chaired the session.

In April 2016, Conducted National English Language Aptitude test 2016 in association with Colorado Height University Denver, where almost 10,000 students from schools and universities across India participated for English Grammar Proficiency.

On 3rd September 2016, International Seminar on Indian Philosophical foundation for modern education, focusing on the connect between Gurukul Shiksha and formal education was organised. It was attended by Ambassadors from Czech Republic and Jordan session chaired by Dr. Jose Luis Chief Patron, Foundation for Vedic Indian Corporation Chief Guest of the Event was Sh. Manish Sisodia, Deputy CM (Delhi)

In November 2016, Co-chaired Session of AICTE sponsored FDP program on values and ethics at Dewan Group of Institutions, Meerut.

January 2017, CEE signed up an MOU with Maharishi University Malta to develop Vedic Science course in India. The program is underway to be launched in India.
