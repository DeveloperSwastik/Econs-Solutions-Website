About Us

Dewan Tyres Limited is part of the reputed Dewan Vidya Sagar Group, one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of Bias Tyres in India, with a prodigious history and colossal experience of 20 years in manufacturing field. We offers a wide range of products in Agricultural & OTR and Motorcycle / 3-wheeler segments. The company has two manufacturing units- Dewan tyre plant and Dewan tube plant- which are located very close to each other.
